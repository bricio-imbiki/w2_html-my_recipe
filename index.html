My favorite meal recipe

What I like about this recipe : This avocado toast is a quick and easy meal that can be enjoyed for any meal.
How often do I eat it : I enjoy this meal quite frequently, at least once a week.
When was the last time I ate it : The last time I enjoyed this meal was just a few days ago.
Now let's cook!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon of everything bagel seasoning
  • 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation's steps

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocados with a fork until smooth.
  4. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the mashed avocados and mix well.
  5. Spread the avocado mixture evenly on the toasted bread slices.
  6. Sprinkle everything bagel seasoning on top of the avocado toast.
